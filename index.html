Assalamualaikum Warahmatullahi Wa Barakatuhu


By the grace of Almighty Allah and his blessings MADRASA QASIMULULOOM has Progressed in its 23 years of service. After completing its 23rd year in Islamic Education and Social Services we feel Honoured and satisfied to present briefly its activities, progress, future programmes and requirements. On this opportunity, I offer my Sincere gratitude to Almighty Allah Subhanahu Ta'ala, as well as to all brothers for their sincere cooperation and contribution.


Indeed by the mercy and grace of Allah as well as the sincere cooperation of all brothers, Madrasa Qasimul Uloom is Progressing towards its Promising future. This Madrasa functions under a registered TRUST which was founded by (Maulana) Abdul Haleem Siddiqui.

About Our Founders:

In the mid seventies (Maulana) Abdul Haleem Siddiqui completed his Master's degree in English Literature and (Maulana) Jamshed Ahmed (Vakil Sahab) Completed his degree in Law from Allahabad University in U.P., India. During these days in Allahabad they met Janab Qari Habeeb Ahmed Sahab (R.A.) who guided them towards the way of life taught by our beloved prophet (S.A.W.).

After their education was completed they worked in Mumbai for a short period in MANIAR BROTHERS until with Allah's wish their inner self-guided them towards the way of The Sunnah, Which Janab Qari Sahab (R.A.) invited them to during their days in Allahabad. They established contact with him and with his advice they both left Mumbai for Jalalabaad. There they gained Islamic knowledge at Madrasa Miftahul Uloom under the guidance of Hazrat Maulana Masiullah Khan Sahab (R.A.). For seven years they studied at the Madrasa and were bestowed with the honour to serve Hazrat Maulana Masiullah Khan Sahab (R.A).

During the end of their seventh year Janab Qari M. Siddique Ahmed Sahab Bandvi (R.A.) persistently brought forward the need to start a Madrasa at Pratapgarh. He insisted that (Maulana) Jamshed Ahmed and (Maulana) Abdul Haleem take up this task. This is when keeping their Trust on Allah they started this Madrasa with only seven students. This centre was named Madrasa Qasimul Uloom. It served as a centre from where they started and monitored Islamic Educational activities in villages in and around Pratapgarh, Jaunpur and Sultanpur. They started teaching in villages where Bid'ah were common and Islamic knowledge was almost nil among the common people. With Allah's wish today this Madrasa offers Islamic education to 3028 students across 72 villages !

All Praise to Allah!

Our Activities


  • Makatib:
    Makatib are small Islamic schools usually under a temporary roof in the adjoining villages of Pratapgarh, Sultanpur and Jaunpur. The Madrasa currently operates 72 Makatib spread out in these three districts. About 3028 students attend these Makatib. All teaching in Makatib is during non-school hours, so that school and college students can benefit from this facility.

    Management of Taraweeh:
    Every year the Madrasa sends Huffaz (Those who can recite Qur'aan by memory) to distant areas for Taraweeh Salaat, in the month of Ramadhaan. No fees are taken in this connection all the Huffaz are sent to their fixed areas with caution and emphasis. The villagers are also instructed they should not give anything to the Reciters of Taraweeh by way of gratitude, reward, offerings or presents.

    Management of Qurbani (Annual Sacrifice):
    Every year, in the month of Eid-al-Adhaa, Qurbani (Annual Sacrifice) is arranged by Madrasa Qasim-ul-Uloom. The management of Qurbani is carried out under the supervision of officials responsible to the Madrasa. The sacrificial animals are bought by these officials who are accompanied by some teachers of the Madrasa experienced in such matters.

    Installation of Borewells:
    Most homes of sorrounding villages do not have direct access to clean water. Keeping our trust in Allah we decided to install manual and electric motor water pumps according to the need of the area.
